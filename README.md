Vagabond.ai | Live AI Travel Concierge

A premium, high-performance travel discovery tool built with modern web technologies. Vagabond.ai provides real-time destination insights, weather forecasts, currency conversion, and local points of interest based on your "Travel Vibe."

Geospatial Tracking: Interactive mapping powered by Leaflet.js, featuring high-contrast light and dark modes for varied environments.

Proximity Discovery: A smart radius-based search system (0.5 to 75 miles/km) to find hidden landmarks, parks, and amenities around your base.

Travel Inventory: A dynamic, session-based packing list that updates its recommendations based on your "Trip Vibe."

Financial & Climate Data: Real-time visibility into major currency exchange rates and local weather forecasts.

üìñ Quick Start Guide: How to Use

Set Your Destination: Use the main search bar to find a city. The dashboard will automatically update the map, weather, and currency exchange for that specific region.

Choose Your Vibe: Select a category (Culture, Nature, Food, etc.) to tailor the discovery engine to your interests.

Adjust the Discovery Radius: Use the settings slider to expand or contract your search area. This determines how far the "Discovery Engine" looks for points of interest.

Manage Your Gear: Use the sidebar to track your packing progress. The list will suggest items based on the "Vibe" you selected earlier.

Book Your Route: Switch to the "Flight Search" tab to quickly generate search links for major aggregators based on your current inputs.
